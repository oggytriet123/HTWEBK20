LSM400 Linear Smith Machine

Linear Smith Machines are the ultimate in weight machines and the LSM400 leads the field. Designed for both serious fitness users and beginners, the LSM400 provides safety, control, and effective resistance training.

Lock points can be set to prevent the bar from moving outside your body's natural range of motion. You don't even need a workout partner.

Features

  • Smooth Smith Action: Bar slides on Teflon bushings.
  • Proper Angle: 7 degree lifting path prevents injury.
  • Safety: 20 lock-out points.
  • Rugged: Heavy 12 gauge steel construction.
  • Warranty: Lifetime guarantee.
